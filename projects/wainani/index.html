Welcome to Tattoo Wainani Studio!

Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is happy to help you create the right tattoo for you. We will work from studio selections, custom artwork you bring to us, or help you design something totally unique and personal. Please feel comfortable in contacting us or stopping by with any questions regarding tattoo procedures. We are dedicated to offering an atmosphere where everyone feels welcome.

Award-winning Professionals

As licensed tattoo artists, we are committed to offering a safe and sanitary environment to all our customers and to ourselves. Every artist is educated in blood borne pathogen, cross contamination and sterilization. Our autoclaves are third-party spore tested weekly. Needles, tubes, and pigment trays are only ever used once. We review aftercare instructions with you carefully and urge you to follow them diligently. Acquiring a new tattoo is an experience filled with excitement and maybe a little apprehension. Please ask any questions you have. We want you to be absolutely happy with your body art.
