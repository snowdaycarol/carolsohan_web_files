About Me

Future front-end developer with strong interest in UI and graphics. In previous lives I studied industrial design and art history, made pottery, waited tables, and managed events. More recently I completed a Web Design Certificate from University of the Arts, and am now making things with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript! I continue to refine my skills, while learning new ones.

I try to be thoughtfully creative in my work, enjoy being part of a team, and want to use my skills to help other people. In 2015 and 2016 I volunteered at WordCamp US and this year started attending Girl Develop It meetups.

I live in Philadelphia with Bill Moriarty, our daughter, and a beagle.
